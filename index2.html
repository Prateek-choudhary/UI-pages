Our 14-day free trial gives you full access to all features of the Pro plan. No credit card required to sign up. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or your account will automatically downgrade to the free plan.

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new rate will be charged immediately. If you downgrade, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. You only pay the monthly or annual subscription fee.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. For Enterprise plans, we also offer invoicing.
